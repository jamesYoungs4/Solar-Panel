Common Misconceptions YOU May Have!?

You might believe green energy is unreliable and expensive, but...
  • modern grids use storage, forecasting, and diverse energy sources to provide reliability.
  • prices for wind, solar, and storage have dropped dramatically.
  • clean energy is now often cheaper than fossil fuels over the full lifecycle.
You might think solar only works in sunny, hot climates; and don't work at all in winter, but...
  • solar panels work on cloudy or cool days and often perform better in lower temperatures.
  • snow may slow output temporarily, but it isn't something to worry about in the long term.
