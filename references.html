Brian's Notes

1. Environmental Benefits Produces electricity without burning fossil fuels, cutting CO₂ and pollution. Helps slow climate change and reduces air and water contamination. Renewable, abundant, and has a smaller ecological footprint than fossil fuels.
2. Financial & Economic Benefits Lowers electricity bills and provides long-term energy cost stability. Creates jobs in manufacturing, installation, and maintenance. Attracts investment and requires little maintenance over its lifespan.
3. Social & Public Health Benefits Expands energy access, especially in remote or underserved areas. Improves public health by reducing pollution-related illnesses. Increases community resilience when paired with battery storage.
4. Technology & Infrastructure Benefits Drives innovation in batteries, smart grids, and energy systems. Enables distributed energy generation, improving grid reliability. Scalable for homes, businesses, or large solar farms.
5. Global & Long-Term Benefits Supports the transition to clean energy worldwide. Improves energy security and reduces dependence on fossil fuels. Contributes to sustainable development and long-term environmental protection.
